Foodteria

Quick Service, Full Service & Retail

Our top recommended POS for the food industry. Montreal-based system covering quick service restaurants, full service dining, and retail operations including dépanneurs. Features self-order kiosks, seamless Global Payments terminal integration, real-time order management, inventory tracking, and kitchen display systems built for high-volume environments.

Colossale

Omnichannel: Restaurant & Retail

Cloud POS by SDP Inc. with built-in e-commerce and Global Payments terminal compatibility. Serves both restaurant and retail verticals with unified omnichannel management. Among the first systems certified MEV-SRM by Revenu Québec.

Auphan

Hospitality & Fine Dining

Hospitality-focused hybrid POS that works both online and offline. Connects natively with Global Payments and Clover terminals, so payment processing runs smoothly without extra configuration. Designed for hotels, restaurants, and bars with fine dining coursing, pay-at-table, and multi-location management.

Cluster

Restaurant Platform

Restaurant-first platform processing over $2B in recurring sales annually. Compatible with Global Payments terminals for seamless payment integration. Features online ordering, delivery, and cloud reporting. WEB-SRM certified with 200+ verified reviews.

Genius Restaurant

All-in-One Restaurant

All-in-one restaurant POS by Global Payments. Countertop and handheld terminals with kitchen sync, real-time reporting, and seamless payment integration for any restaurant size.

Genius Enterprise

Multi-Location Enterprise

Cloud-native POS for multi-location enterprises by Global Payments. Unified commerce engine with centralized management, advanced analytics, and global deployment capabilities.
